Sheffield Ubuntu Notes: 4th Edition

Recent update is on: Accessing Novell filestore.
Ubuntu is a flavour of Linux which has grown rapidly in popularity and usage over the recent years. We believe that UBUNTU workstations are good alternative to Windows workstations particularly for students and staff carrying out computational research at Sheffield University.
The Sheffield University IT infrastructure supports Novell Networks environment with many of the staff and student machines running Windows (mainly XP) workstations.
Ubuntu workstations can happily integrate and live alongside this environment without any loss of functionality. The intention of these pages is to provide a template towards achieving such an objective not just at Sheffield University but other similar establishments.
